Craftcy: marketplace for artists
A case study project to design a responsive marketplace platform for artist and their customers in Cyprus.
ROLE: UX/UI designer designing from conception to delivery.
01. PROJECT OVERVIEW
Problem
The art marketplace industry in Cyprus for ceramics, candles, and paintings has experienced steady growth over the past few years. The demand for unique and handmade art pieces is increasing, presenting a significant opportunity for market expansion. Local artists in Cyprus face difficulties in finding a suitable platform to sell their handmade products, while buyers who are interested in purchasing unique and locally crafted pieces for their homes have limited options to access such products.
Solution
Design a marketplace platform that enables individual artists in Cyprus to easily and effectively showcase and sell their handmade products and provide a seamless shopping experience for customers who are looking for unique and locally-made tableware for their homes.
02. RESEARCH
User research
To create a human-centred interface and know about users paint points and delights, based on competitors and market research, were conducted 8 in-depth interviews with local artists and art lovers.

Pain

Customers have a difficulty in discovering new and unique art pieces among the vast selection.

Solution

Enhance the browsing experience with advanced search and filtering options, personalized recommendations based on customer preferences.

Pain

Artists have limited exposure for artwork, struggling to reach a wider audience.

Solution

Provide artists with comprehensive and visually appealing profiles that showcase their portfolio, background, and artistic process, helping them to stand out and build credibility.

Pain

Concerns about the authenticity and quality of handmade artworks when purchasing online.

Solution

Showcase customer reviews and ratings, implement a rigorous verification process to ensure the authenticity of handmade artworks, display detailed artist profiles and bios.

Insight

Artists and customers express a desire to promote and support local art in Cyprus.

Solution

Highlights local artists and their works, organizing virtual events, and partnering with local art organizations to foster a sense of community support and encourage the purchase of local art.

Customer personas
Upon target audience research, the main personas were created for more empathetic design process
03. DESIGN PROCCESS
Sitemap
Recognizing that users were facing difficulties in finding their way around, I developed a user-friendly sitemap. By making strategic information architecture decisions, I aimed to simplify the browsing journey and enhance overall usability.

04. UI Design
Homepage
Homepage showcases a prominent search bar with powerful filtering options, empowering users to find exactly what they're looking for. In the mobile version of the homepage, the focus is on showcasing the main shopping categories in order to engage users and spark their curiosity to explore further.
Search results page
The search results are displayed in a grid layout, allowing users to easily scan through the artworks and quickly find what they're looking for. Each item is accompanied by essential information such as the artist's name, price, and a brief description. Also users can easily add an interesting product to their basket or add to favourites to buy later.
Product page
The page includes a detailed description that highlights the inspiration, techniques, artist description and materials used in creating the artwork. User reviews and ratings are prominently displayed on the product page to allows potential buyers to gain insights from others who have purchased and experienced the artwork firsthand.
